# FleetOS v1.1 Roadmap

**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Codename:** Vehicle Intelligence & Operational Control  
**Base version:** 1.0.1  
**Status:** Planning

## Background

FleetOS v1.0 established core operational platforms:

- **Compliance** — document lifecycle, expiration engine, notifications, analytics
- **Maintenance** — CRUD, scheduler, alerts, analytics
- **Tire** — inventory, history, seasonal planning, alerts, analytics
- **Audit / Governance** — audit logs, change history, diff engine, executive dashboard, analytics
- **Release / Production** — RC-1, PRD-1, REL-1 cleanup, v1.0.1 production tag

v1.0 delivered module-based fleet operations. v1.1 shifts focus to vehicle-level intelligence.

## v1.1 Theme

**Vehicle Intelligence & Operational Control**

FleetOS should evolve from module-based management to unified vehicle intelligence — giving executives a single operational view per vehicle.

## Strategic Objective

Turn vehicle-level data from compliance, maintenance, tire, audit, and financial modules into actionable executive intelligence:

- Which vehicles are healthy vs at risk?
- What is the unified operational timeline for a vehicle?
- Where do cost, compliance risk, and profitability intersect?
- Which vehicles need immediate action?

## Planned Phases

### V11-PLN-1 Roadmap Planning

Planning only. Roadmap metadata, documentation, planning service, roadmap page, and validation.

### VI-1 Vehicle Intelligence Foundation

Create vehicle intelligence service that aggregates vehicle data from existing modules without rewriting module architecture.

### VI-2 Vehicle Health Score

Calculate vehicle health based on compliance status, maintenance plan risk, tire status, alerts, and audit signals.

### VI-3 Vehicle Operational Timeline

Create unified vehicle timeline:

- compliance events
- maintenance events
- tire events
- audit events
- financial events if available

### VI-4 Vehicle Profit/Risk Fusion

Combine cost, maintenance, tire, compliance risk, and profitability into a vehicle-level operational score.

### VI-5 Executive Vehicle Intelligence Dashboard

Create executive view:

- best/worst vehicles
- high-risk vehicles
- high-cost vehicles
- incomplete data vehicles
- action priorities

### STB-2 Stabilization

v1.1 QA sprint for navigation, regression, empty states, and release readiness.

### RC-2 Release Candidate

v1.1 RC preparation with inventory, known issues, and validation.

### PRD-2 Production Release

v1.1 production preparation and certification.

## Explicitly Out of Scope for v1.1

- Multi-user RBAC
- External notification channels
- Mobile app
- Driver management
- Advanced fuel/HGS intelligence
- Export/reporting engine
- Approval workflow

These can be v1.2+.
